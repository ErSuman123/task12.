Explore the Planets

Our solar system consists of eight planets orbiting the Sun. From the scorching surface of Mercury to the icy plains of Neptune, each world offers unique characteristics and mysteries to uncover.

  • Hottest: Venus (464°C)
  • Coldest: Uranus (-224°C)
  • Largest: Jupiter (318 Earth masses)
  • Tallest mountain: Olympus Mons on Mars (22 km)
